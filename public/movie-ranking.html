Alejandro's Favorite Cinematic Adventures

My Top 3 Favorite Movies of all Time

The Godfather

An amazing mob story that spanned throughout 3 movies with the first installment being the best
(in my opinion, of course). Al Pacino and Marlon Brando give legendary performances alongside James Caan
and Diane Keaton.

Scarface

Another amazing performance by Al Pacino in which he plays a Cuban refugee just arrived to Miami in the 80's and
makes the world his by dominating the drug world.

The Naked Gun (All Three Installments)

In a total 180 from the previous 2 movies, The Naked Gun is an ridiculous slapstick style comedy by
The Zucker Brothers and stars Leslie Nielsen. Nielsen plays Lieutenant Frank Drebin who is the head detective for
Police Squad and gets into many hijinks during his investigations. Also, watch out for stupid jokes in the dialogue
that will definitely get a laugh out of you.
